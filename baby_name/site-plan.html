Overview

Purpose

This site is meant for new parents. We want to ease up the burden and the stress of finding an adequate name for their baby. Our webpage mixes up different name within our list with your Last Name until you find the adequate one.

Audience

This webpage is meant for young adults, who just became parents.

Dynamic elements

We will have a list of different names for both genders. The parents will add their last name and from which gender they want to try a name for. The button for each gender will call a first name and it will be displayed in a box with their lastname on it.
